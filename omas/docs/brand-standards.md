# Brand Standards

This document defines usage rules, accessibility standards, technical requirements, and applications for the Omas Coffee brand.

## Quick Start
Consult this guide when reviewing vendor work, ensuring compliance, preparing files for production, or establishing partnerships. This is your reference for maintaining brand integrity.

---

## Brand Usage Rules

### Logo Clear Space
- **Minimum:** Equal to height of "O" in "Omas" on all sides
- **Ideal:** 1.5x the height of "O" for breathing room
- **Never:** Place logo touching edges or other elements

### Minimum Size Requirements
- **Print:** 0.75 inch width minimum (maintaining legibility)
- **Digital:** 120px width minimum for web
- **Favicon:** 32x32px (use simplified secondary mark)
- **Social Media Profile:** 400x400px minimum

### Improper Uses - DO NOT:
- Stretch, condense, or distort logo proportions
- Rotate logo at angles (always upright or perfectly horizontal)
- Change brand colors (only use approved palette)
- Add drop shadows, glows, or effects to logo
- Place on busy backgrounds without proper contrast
- Retype or recreate logo (always use official files)
- Use low-resolution or pixelated versions
- Outline logo or add unapproved borders

### Approved Color Variations
- Full color on dark backgrounds (primary use)
- Full color on light backgrounds (approved)
- Single color antique gold
- Single color cream (on dark backgrounds)
- Single color black (on light backgrounds only)

### Partner Co-Branding
- Omas Coffee logo always equal or larger size
- Maintain clear space between logos
- Use lockup with vertical divider line if needed
- Approve all co-branded materials before production

### File Formats
- **Vector:** AI, EPS, SVG (preferred for print and scaling)
- **Raster:** PNG with transparency (web use)
- **Color Modes:** RGB for digital, CMYK for print

## Accessibility Standards

### Color Contrast Requirements
- **WCAG AA Minimum:** 4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text (18px+)
- **Our Standards:**
  - Cream (#faf8f3) on Deep Black (#0a0908): 17.2:1 ✓
  - Soft Gold (#f5e6d3) on Deep Walnut (#2c1810): 8.1:1 ✓
  - Antique Gold (#d4af37) on Deep Black (#0a0908): 6.2:1 ✓
- **Test:** All text combinations before deployment

### Minimum Font Sizes
- **Web Body Text:** 16px minimum, 18px preferred
- **Web Small Text:** 14px absolute minimum
- **Print Body Text:** 10pt minimum, 12pt preferred
- **Print Fine Print:** 8pt absolute minimum
- **Menu Boards:** 18px minimum for readability at distance

### Alt Text Guidelines
- **Logo:** "Omas Coffee logo" or "Omas Coffee - Brewed with Memory"
- **Product Photos:** Describe drink + context (e.g., "Cappuccino with latte art on walnut table")
- **Decorative Butterfly:** Leave alt empty (decorative only) or "decorative butterfly"
- **Lifestyle Images:** Describe scene and mood (e.g., "Two people sharing coffee at afternoon table")

### Typography Accessibility
- **Gothic Font:** Use only for logo and large headings (never for body text)
- **Body Text:** Clear sans-serif for maximum readability
- **Line Length:** 50-75 characters per line for optimal reading
- **Line Height:** 1.5 minimum for body text
- **Avoid:** All caps for paragraphs, centered body text

### Interactive Elements
- **Focus States:** Visible outline (2px antique gold) for keyboard navigation
- **Click Targets:** Minimum 44x44px for touch interfaces
- **Form Labels:** Always visible, never placeholder-only
- **Error Messages:** Clear color (not relying on color alone) + text explanation

### Motion & Animation
- Respect prefers-reduced-motion media query
- Provide option to disable animations
- Keep animations subtle and purposeful

## Technical Requirements

### Vector Formats
- Vector format (AI, EPS, SVG)
- Work at small sizes (favicon) and large (signage)
- Strong silhouette for single-color applications
- Must work on both dark and light backgrounds

### File Preparation
- **Print Files:** CMYK color mode, 300 DPI, with bleed
- **Web Files:** RGB color mode, optimized file sizes
- **Logos:** Provide full color, single color, and reversed versions

## Trademark & Copyright Usage

### Trademark Symbol Usage
- **Omas Coffee™:** Use ™ on first occurrence in any document
- **Tagline:** "Mit Erinnerung Gebraut"™ when used as marketing slogan
- **After Registration:** Replace ™ with ® once federally registered

### Copyright Notice
- **Format:** © 2025 Omas Coffee. All rights reserved.
- **Placement:** Footer of websites, back of printed materials
- **Photography:** © 2025 Omas Coffee / [Photographer Name]

### Protecting Our Brand
- Register domain variations (.com, .coffee, .cafe)
- Monitor social media handles across platforms
- Document first use dates for all brand elements
- Maintain brand guidelines version control

## Crisis Communication Guidelines

### Response Principles
- **Speed:** Acknowledge within 2 hours, resolve within 24 hours
- **Tone:** Empathetic, personal, solution-focused
- **Channel:** Match the platform where issue arose
- **Voice:** Owner/family member responds to serious issues

### Template Responses

#### Product Quality Issue
"We're so sorry your experience didn't meet the standard Oma would expect. Please DM us your order details so we can make this right immediately. Your satisfaction is our family tradition."

#### Service Complaint
"This isn't the warm welcome we strive for. We'd love to hear more about your experience and how we can restore your faith in our family business. Please reach out at [contact]."

#### Supply Chain Delay
"Like any family recipe, good things sometimes take time. We're experiencing [issue] but expect to have your favorites back by [date]. Thank you for your patience and loyalty."

### Escalation Path
1. Barista → Shift Lead (minor issues)
2. Shift Lead → Owner (quality/safety concerns)
3. Owner → Legal Counsel (liability issues)
4. Public Response → Private Resolution

## Vendor & Supplier Guidelines

### Approved Vendor Requirements
- Must use provided brand assets (never recreate)
- Submit proofs for approval before production
- Maintain color matching within Delta E of 2
- Sign brand usage agreement

### Quality Standards
- **Print Vendors:** G7 certified preferred
- **Merchandise:** Eco-friendly materials when possible
- **Food Suppliers:** Local when available, quality over cost
- **Packaging:** Sustainability certifications required

### Co-Branding Rules
- Omas Coffee logo minimum 30% larger than partner
- Our brand elements appear first/top/left
- Partner logos in grayscale or single color
- Approval required for all co-branded materials

### Delivery Standards
- Include brand guidelines with every asset delivery
- Provide files in requested formats
- Name files according to: OmasCoffee_[Item]_[Date]_[Version]
- Archive all approved versions

## Brand Consistency Metrics

### Measurement Framework

#### Visual Consistency Score (Monthly)
- Logo usage compliance: Target 100%
- Color accuracy: Delta E < 2
- Typography adherence: 95% minimum
- Photography style match: 90% minimum

#### Voice Consistency (Weekly)
- Tone guide adherence in communications
- German term usage accuracy
- Response time to inquiries
- Customer sentiment analysis

#### Experience Consistency (Daily)
- Product quality scores
- Service standard compliance
- Wait time targets (< 3 minutes)
- Atmosphere checklist completion

### Audit Schedule
- **Daily:** Cart setup checklist
- **Weekly:** Social media review
- **Monthly:** Full brand audit
- **Quarterly:** Vendor compliance check
- **Annually:** Complete brand guide review

### Common Violations to Monitor
- Stretched or distorted logos
- Off-brand color usage
- Inconsistent pricing displays
- Unauthorized photography styles
- Off-tone social media posts
- Non-compliant partner materials

### Correction Process
1. Document violation with screenshot/photo
2. Notify responsible party within 24 hours
3. Provide correct assets and guidelines
4. Set deadline for correction (48 hours typical)
5. Follow up to confirm resolution
6. Update training if pattern emerges
