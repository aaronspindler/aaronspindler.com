# Omas Coffee Design Documentation

Welcome to the Omas Coffee brand and design documentation. This comprehensive guide covers all aspects of the brand identity, visual design, and application guidelines.

---

## Quick Overview

**Omas Coffee** is a premium coffee cart honoring the memory of the owner's German grandmother (Oma), representing the warmth of German coffee tradition and the afternoon "Kaffeezeit" ritual.

**Tagline:** "Mit Erinnerung Gebraut" (Brewed with Memory)

**Core Elements:**
- Gold butterfly motif (Oma's kitchen window memory)
- Walnut wood aesthetic (family cutting board)
- Gothic typography for brand name
- Warm, nostalgic color palette (golds and walnuts)

## Design Principles Summary

### Visual Identity
- **Sophisticated & Elegant** - Not rustic or folksy
- **Modern with Heritage** - Contemporary execution of traditional elements
- **Premium Feel** - Works on dark backgrounds
- **Emotional Warmth** - Honoring memory while welcoming customers

### Color Philosophy
- **Primary:** Antique gold (#d4af37) and deep walnut (#2c1810)
- **Supporting:** Cream (#faf8f3) and soft gold (#f5e6d3)
- **Always:** High contrast, warm tones, premium feel

### Typography Approach
- **Logo:** Gothic/Blackletter for "Omas Coffee" (heritage connection)
- **Body:** Clean sans-serif (readability and modernity)
- **Balance:** Traditional elegance meets contemporary clarity

### Key Symbols
- **Gold Butterfly:** Oma's memory, elegance, transformation
- **Walnut Wood:** Family tradition, warmth, craftsmanship
- **Circular Badge:** German guild tradition, established quality
