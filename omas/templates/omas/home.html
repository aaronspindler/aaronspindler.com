Our Story

The Kaffeezeit Tradition

Every afternoon at 3 PM, German families pause for Kaffeezeit—a cherished ritual where coffee, cake, and conversation create moments that matter. At Omas Coffee, we honor this tradition through the memory of our owner's grandmother, whose kitchen welcomed all with the warmth of fresh-brewed coffee and the gentle dance of a golden butterfly at her window.

That weathered walnut cutting board, worn smooth by generations of loving hands preparing Streuselkuchen, inspires every cup we serve. We bring you not just coffee, but a moment of Gemütlichkeit—that untranslatable feeling of warmth, belonging, and contentment.
