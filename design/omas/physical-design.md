# Physical Design

This document provides specifications for all physical applications, including the coffee cart, menu board, packaging, and print materials.

## Quick Start
Reference this guide when designing the physical cart, creating packaging, developing print materials, or planning the tangible brand experience. Ensures consistency across all physical touchpoints.

---

## Cart Design Direction

### Cart Dimensions (Estimated)
- **Overall Size:** 6ft wide × 3ft deep × 7ft tall (with signage)
- **Counter Height:** 36-40 inches (comfortable serving height)
- **Service Window:** 4ft wide × 2ft tall
- **Storage Below:** 30 inches height for equipment
- **Mobility:** Must fit through standard 36" doorways when turned

### Physical Cart Aesthetic
- **Primary Color:** Deep walnut brown (stained wood or painted finish)
- **Accent Color:** Antique gold metal hardware and trim
- **Counter Surface:** Natural walnut wood top (sealed for durability)
- **Base/Wheels:** Matte black industrial casters (5" heavy-duty, lockable)

### Materials
- **Structure:** Powder-coated steel frame in deep black or walnut color
- **Panels:** Wood panels in rich walnut stain
- **Hardware:** Brass or antique gold finish
- **Countertop:** Sealed walnut or walnut-stained hardwood

### Signage Placement
- **Front Panel:** Primary logo (12-18 inches wide)
- **Side Panels:** Tagline or "Coffee Served Here" with butterfly icon
- **Top Sign:** Freestanding A-frame or mounted sign with logo + "Open"
- **All Signs:** Backlit or well-lit for visibility

### Menu Board Integration
- **Placement:** Side-mounted swing-out board or freestanding beside cart
- **Size:** 24x36 inches minimum for readability
- **Material:** Chalkboard with gold frame or printed board in dark walnut frame
- **Lighting:** Small LED spotlights if evening service

### Branding Placement
- **Logo:** Front center panel (most visible position)
- **Badge/Seal:** Side panels or top corners
- **Tagline:** Below logo or on side panels
- **Website/Social:** Small text on lower side panels

### Functional Elements
- **Equipment:** Espresso machine, grinder prominently displayed
- **Storage:** Concealed storage with wood panel doors
- **Display:** Glass or acrylic case for pastries with gold frame
- **Details:** Leather straps, vintage clips, brass hooks for towels

### Atmosphere Elements
- Small potted plants (herbs or flowers)
- Vintage scales or coffee equipment on display
- Warm Edison bulb string lights above
- Small butterfly ornaments or oak leaf accents

## Menu Board Design

### Layout
- **Structure:** Three-column grid (drink name, description, price)
- **Hierarchy:** Category headers in gothic font, items in sans-serif
- **Alignment:** Left-aligned names, right-aligned prices, centered descriptions

### Typography
- **Category Headers:** Gothic font, 28-32px, antique gold color
- **Item Names:** Sans-serif bold, 18-20px, cream color
- **Descriptions:** Sans-serif regular, 14-16px, soft gold, italic
- **Prices:** Sans-serif medium, 18-20px, cream color

### Visual Elements
- **Dividers:** Thin antique gold lines (1-2px) between categories
- **Ornaments:** Small butterfly or oak leaf accents at section breaks
- **Background:** Deep walnut or deep black
- **Border:** Double-ring border (like badge design) in antique gold

### Content Organization
- **Sections:** Coffee, Espresso Drinks, Specialty Drinks, Pastries
- **Daily Special:** Highlighted box with bright gold border
- **Seasonal Items:** Marked with small oak leaf icon

### Readability
- High contrast (cream/soft gold text on dark backgrounds)
- Adequate spacing between items (12-16px)
- Clear price alignment
- Readable from 3-6 feet away
- Test legibility in various lighting conditions

## Packaging Specifications

### Cups (Primary Serving Vessels)
- **Material:** Double-wall paper cups, fully compostable
- **Color:** Deep walnut brown or white with printed design
- **Design Elements:**
  - Logo on front (2 inches wide)
  - Subtle butterfly pattern wrapping around
  - "Mit Liebe Gebraut" near rim
- **Sizes:** 8oz (small), 12oz (medium), 16oz (large)
- **Lids:** Compostable white or brown, sip-through design

### Cup Sleeves
- **Material:** Recycled kraft cardboard
- **Color:** Natural kraft with single-color print (antique gold)
- **Design:** Logo centered, tagline below, small butterfly motif
- **Size:** Standard sizes (8oz, 12oz, 16oz)

### To-Go Bags
- **Material:** Natural kraft paper
- **Color:** Natural with antique gold foil stamping
- **Design:** Logo on front, tagline on bottom, simple and clean
- **Handle:** Matching kraft twisted handles

### Pastry Boxes
- **Material:** White cardboard or natural kraft
- **Design:** Badge/seal version of logo on top
- **Closure:** Simple fold-over with circular gold sticker
- **Size:** Multiple sizes for 1-6 pastries

### Napkins
- **Material:** Recycled paper napkins
- **Color:** Natural kraft or cream
- **Print:** Small logo or butterfly icon in corner (single color)
- **Fold:** Quarter fold or interfold

### Stickers
- **Die-Cut:** Circular badge design or butterfly shape
- **Material:** Matte finish vinyl
- **Uses:** Bag seals, laptop stickers, promotional giveaways
- **Size:** 2-3 inch diameter for circular, various for butterfly

### Sustainability Note
- All packaging should be recyclable or compostable
- Minimal plastic use
- Highlight eco-friendly choices with small leaf icon

## Print Materials Details

### Business Cards
- **Size:** Standard 3.5 x 2 inches
- **Orientation:** Horizontal
- **Front:** Logo centered, tagline below, deep walnut background
- **Back:** Contact info (phone, website, social) in cream text, small butterfly watermark
- **Finish:** Matte with soft-touch coating
- **Special:** Antique gold foil for logo

### Loyalty/Punch Card
- **Size:** Credit card size (3.375 x 2.125 inches)
- **Front:** "Kaffeezeit Club" header, 10 coffee cup icons to stamp, logo at bottom
- **Back:** Terms and contact info
- **Material:** Thick cardstock (16pt)
- **Special:** Gold foil stamps or physical hole punch

### Flyers & Promotional Materials
- **Standard Size:** 5.5 x 8.5 inches (half letter)
- **Layout:** Logo top, main message center, call-to-action bottom
- **Background:** Dark with gold accents or cream with walnut accents
- **Imagery:** Product photo or lifestyle image
- **QR Code:** Gold-colored QR code linking to website/social

### Gift Cards
- **Size:** Credit card size
- **Design:** Circular badge on front, deep walnut background
- **Back:** Instructions, website, card number
- **Material:** PVC card with magnetic stripe or scratch-off code
- **Packaging:** Small kraft envelope with gold seal sticker

### Posters/Signage
- **Large Format:** 18x24 or 24x36 inches for cart area
- **Content:** Seasonal specials, origin story, coffee education
- **Style:** Minimal text, large imagery, bold typography
- **Frame:** Dark walnut or matte black frames

### Print Specifications
- **Color Mode:** CMYK
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI minimum
- **Bleed:** 0.125 inch bleed on all sides
- **Safe Area:** 0.25 inch inside trim for important elements

## Staff Uniforms & Appearance

### Apron Design
- **Style:** Bib apron or waist apron (barista choice)
- **Color:** Natural canvas or deep walnut brown
- **Material:** Heavy cotton canvas or denim
- **Logo Placement:** Embroidered on chest (bib) or pocket (waist)
- **Embroidery:** Antique gold thread for logo and name

### Clothing Guidelines
- **Shirt:** White, cream, or black - clean and pressed
- **Alternative:** Omas Coffee branded t-shirt (black with gold logo)
- **Bottoms:** Dark jeans or black pants, no rips
- **Shoes:** Closed-toe, non-slip, black or brown

### Accessories
- **Hat Options:** Black or brown cap with embroidered butterfly
- **Name Badge:** Wood-grain badge with gold engraving
- **Optional Bow Tie:** Deep walnut or antique gold for special events

### Grooming Standards
- Hair tied back if long
- Minimal jewelry for safety
- Natural, welcoming appearance
- Clean, trimmed fingernails

## Point of Sale & Service Items

### POS System
- **Hardware:** Square Stand or similar in white or black
- **Screen Facing Customer:** Display logo during idle
- **Receipt Paper:** Kraft brown if available
- **Receipt Design:** Logo header, "Danke schön!" footer

### Tip Jar
- **Style:** Vintage mason jar or ceramic crock
- **Label:** "Kaffeegeld" (coffee money) in gothic font
- **Decoration:** Tied with burlap and antique gold ribbon
- **Sign:** Small tent card explaining tips support local charity

### Service Accessories
- **Napkin Dispenser:** Antique gold or dark wood
- **Stirrers:** Wooden, stamped with butterfly if custom
- **Sugar/Cream Station:** Vintage wooden tray setup
- **Water Pitcher:** Glass with "Wasser" etched or labeled

## Seasonal Decorations

### Approach
- Subtle seasonal touches that don't overwhelm brand
- Natural elements preferred (real flowers, branches)
- Maintain sophisticated, not kitschy aesthetic

### Spring
- Fresh flowers in vintage milk bottles
- Pastel butterfly accents
- Easter: Subtle egg and flower arrangements

### Summer
- Potted herbs (basil, mint for drinks)
- Golden sunflower accents
- Light fabric bunting in brand colors

### Autumn
- Real pumpkins and gourds
- Dried oak leaves and acorns
- Amber glass votives

### Winter
- Pine branches with gold-dusted pinecones
- White twinkle lights (warm, not cool)
- Cinnamon stick bundles

### Holiday-Specific
- **Oktoberfest:** Subtle - blue and white checkered accent only
- **Christmas:** Traditional German market style - natural, candlelit
- **Valentine's:** Heart-shaped Lebkuchen display
